Staining / Protect

Proper staining is an important maintenance to keep your fence protected from the elements and save home owners the cost of replacement in the long run. In the hot Texas weather, its recommended to stain your wood fence every 3-5 years. Unprotected wood can become susceptible to mold and mildew which decreases the life of the fence.
