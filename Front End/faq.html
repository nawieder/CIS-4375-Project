Frequently Asked Questions

Do you handle permits and regulations?

Answer: All city permits are handled by us, while HOA request/approvals must be submitted by the resident.

Does your company use concrete when installing fencing?

Answer: Yes we do. We use concrete whenever we install a vinyl fence, an aluminum fence or a chain link fence. The only exception is with wood fencing – please check with your local office to determine whether concrete is used on all, some, or none of the wood fence posts. Other than this exception, we include concrete on each post that is installed.

How long does it take to install a Fence or Gate?

Answer: Installation time depends on the project size and typically takes 1-2 weeks for residential fences. However, the timeline may be extended if city approval and necessary permits are required.

At what depth do you set fence post?

Answer: Typically a 6′ fence post is set at 24″deep in concrete, 7′ fence 32″in concrete and 8′ is set 36″ in concrete.

Do you offer stain or paint for my fence?

Answer: Yes, we offer stain for both new and older fences.

Do I need to be home during the installation?

Answer: You can explore other sections of this website, such as the "About Us" page or our blog, to learn more about our company, mission, and team.

Can you help with fence repair?

Answer: Absolutely! We offer repair services for damaged or aging fences, ensuring they remain secure and functional.

Do you provide custom fencing solutions?

Answer: Yes, we tailor fencing projects to your specific needs, including custom designs and materials.
